Biography

I am a research fellow in the Vlachos lab at BIDMC in the Department of Pathology. My research interests lie in integrating single-cell and bulk data and curated gene sets to better interpret genomic data.

I received my PhD in the Department of Biostatistics at Harvard University where I was advised by Rafael Irizarry and Winston Hide.
